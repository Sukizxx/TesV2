# PHANTOM - Cinematic 3D Animation

A cinematic 3D car showcase with super smooth animations, dramatic lighting, and particle effects.

## Features

- **3D Car Model**: Stylized luxury car with metallic materials
- **Smooth Animations**: Floating effect, camera orbit, and dynamic lighting
- **Cinematic Effects**: Vignette, particles, and text overlays
- **Interactive Controls**: Toggle rotation and change car colors
- **Responsive Design**: Works on all screen sizes

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Watch the cinematic 3D animation unfold
3. Use the controls in the bottom-right to:
   - Toggle car rotation
   - Change car color scheme

## Animation Sequence

The animation cycles through different text overlays:
1. **PHANTOM** - Brand reveal
2. **0-100** - Performance stat (2.9 seconds)
3. **1200** - Horsepower showcase
4. **EXPERIENCE** - Future vision

## Technical Details

- **3D Engine**: Three.js
- **Lighting**: Multi-source cinematic lighting (directional, spot, point)
- **Materials**: PBR materials with high metalness and low roughness
- **Post-processing**: Radial vignette effect
- **Particles**: Floating ambient particles with glow

## Color Schemes

The car can cycle through 4 color schemes:
- Dark Navy with Red accent
- Deep Blue with Cyan accent
- Purple with Electric Blue accent
- Royal Blue with Pink accent

- Chrome/Edge (recommended)
